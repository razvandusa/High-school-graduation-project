What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a game made up of blocks, creatures, and community. Blocks can be used to reshape the world or build fantastical creations. Creatures can be battled or befriended.

Is it good for the kids?

Absolutely! It offers many benefits for children, including promoting creativity, problem-solving skills, and social interaction.
